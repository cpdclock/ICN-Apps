Customizable Reports: A new feature added to our tool

A new reporting feature with personalized functionalities for you to create beautiful and customized monitoring reports with clear and actionable insights!

3 Types of Standard Reports

Top Mentions Report

This report reflects the top mentions (brand names, keywords, hashtags) across all the digital channels, and analyses their sentiments.

The Top Mentions report also comes in a plain or a more colorful version.

Share of Voice

The Share of Voice report draws the comparison between your brand and the competitors based on the targeted keywords, share of sentiments, online presence, and countries from where your competitors get most of the mentions.

Influencers Report

This report lets you evaluate the results of campaigns running by the influencers using your keywords, product or brand name at one digital channel or the other on selected dates. You can also detect the location and languages of the mentions.
